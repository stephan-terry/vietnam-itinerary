12:30 PM Arrive in Hanoi
Land at Noi Bai International Airport. Allow time for immigration, luggage pickup, and getting connected before heading into the city.
 —
1:30 PM Grab to Hanoi Old Quarter
Take a Grab from the airport to the hotel in the Old Quarter. Estimated travel time is 40–50 minutes depending on traffic.
 $12–18
2:15 PM Check-in — Hanoi Amorita Boutique Hotel & Travel
3 rooms booked for the group. One-night stay before checking out the next day and leaving luggage at the hotel while exploring Hanoi.
 $80 total
3:00 PM Late Lunch — Bun Cha Huong Lien
24 Le Van Huu St · Map
Famous as the spot where Obama and Anthony Bourdain ate in 2016. Grilled pork, noodles, herbs, and dipping broth.
 $3–5
4:00 PM Hoan Kiem Lake Stroll
Map
Walk around the lake, take in the energy of central Hanoi, and see Ngoc Son Temple and the red bridge from the shoreline.
 Free
5:30 PM Hanoi Train Street
Map
Stop by one of Hanoi's most famous streets for coffee or a beer while waiting for the train to pass through the narrow café-lined alley.
 $3–5
7:00 PM Dinner — Pho 10 Ly Quoc Su
10 Ly Quoc Su St · Map
Classic Hanoi pho for dinner. Simple, quick, and a great first-night meal after a long travel day.
 $2–4
8:30 PM Ta Hien Beer Street
Map
The liveliest nightlife strip in the Old Quarter. Cheap beers, plastic stools, backpacker crowd, and a fun first night without overcommitting after the flight.
 $10–15
Daily Total: $110–127 total for the group + food/drinks

Day 1 Tips

  • Stay in the Old Quarter for maximum walkability.
  • Use Grab instead of airport taxis.
  • Keep the first day light and flexible after the flight.
  • The hotel can store luggage after checkout before the Sapa bus.

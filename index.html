Essential Travel Info

Visa & Entry

  • Most nationalities can get an e-Visa online at evisa.gov.vn ($25, 90 days)
  • Apply at least 3 business days before travel
  • Your passport must be valid for 6+ months beyond entry date

SIM & Connectivity

  • Buy a Viettel or Vietnamobile SIM at the airport (~$5 for 10 GB data, 30 days)
  • Bring your unlocked phone
  • Wi-Fi is reliable in most hotels and caf├Ѓ┬Еs

Money

  • Withdraw VND from ATMs (Vietcombank has lowest fees)
  • Carry small bills ├бРѓгРђЮ street vendors rarely have change
  • Credit cards accepted at hotels and upscale restaurants
  • $1 USD = 24,000 VND (check live rate on arrival)

Health & Safety

  • Only drink bottled water ├бРѓгРђЮ avoid ice at unlicensed street stalls
  • Apply DEET mosquito repellent at dusk
  • Travel insurance is strongly recommended
  • Keep copies of your passport and visa separate from the originals
